Messagram forwards your SMS messages to Telegram, allowing you to access your text messages from anywhere, on any device.

Features

Automatic Forwarding

Automatically forwards SMS messages to your Telegram account in real-time.

Multi-SIM Support

Works with dual SIM phones, allowing you to forward messages from both SIM cards.

Contact Integration

Shows contact names instead of just phone numbers for a better messaging experience.

Secure Connection

Connects securely to your Telegram account through Telegram's official bot API.

How It Works

1

Install Messagram

Download and install Messagram from the Google Play Store.

2

Connect to Telegram

Tap "Connect to Telegram" and follow the instructions to connect with the Messagram bot.

3

Grant Permissions

Grant the necessary permissions for SMS access and contacts (optional).

4

Start Receiving Messages

That's it! Your SMS messages will now be forwarded to your Telegram account.
